Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 15, 2025

Ezrae (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information and spiritual conversations. This Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email address, subscription status, and age confirmation.
  • Spiritual Conversations: Text or voice inputs used to provide AI-guided responses.
  • Device Data: IP address, operating system, app version, time zone, and crash logs.
  • Usage Analytics: Interaction data to improve service quality (non-identifiable).
  • Payment Data: Processed securely through Apple; Ezrae never stores card details.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide spiritual and biblical guidance via our AI services.
  • To improve the accuracy and faithfulness of responses.
  • To manage subscriptions, trials, and billing.
  • To ensure platform safety and prevent misuse.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your data. We may share information with trusted processors—such as hosting providers, AI services, or analytics vendors—under strict confidentiality agreements, or as required by law.

4. Retention

We retain account data while your account is active. Conversation data is encrypted and deleted upon account deletion. Analytics data may be stored up to 24 months for product improvement.

5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and safeguards to protect personal data and conversations in transit and at rest.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us below. Deleting your account removes all personal and conversation data from our systems.

7. Children

Ezrae is intended for users aged 13 and older. For minors, parental supervision is encouraged. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Policy as our services evolve. We will notify you of material updates in-app or via email.

9. Contact

To reach our privacy team, use the form above or email privacy@ezrae.app.
